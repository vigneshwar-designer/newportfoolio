Chefy is a simple web app that suggests recipes based on the ingredients you enter. Just type what you have, and it shows dishes you can cook instantly. It's perfect for saving time and reducing food waste.

News pulse...

A web-based news platform designed to organize news into clear categories such as Entertainment, Education, Sports, World News, and more. It also features a regional news system where users can select a specific location to view the latest updates relevant to that area. The platform ensures a seamless experience for accessing both global and local news in one place.
